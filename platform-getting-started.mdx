Welcome to the ArcWeather Platform.

The ArcWeather Platform is a **platform-as-a-service (PaaS)** designed to help organizations and individuals monitor weather, understand risk, and take action — all through a simple, intuitive interface.

This guide provides a high-level overview of what the platform offers and how to get started.

## What is ArcWeather?

ArcWeather is built to turn complex weather data into **clear, actionable insights**.

Instead of working directly with raw data, the platform provides:

- Visual dashboards and map-based views
- Shareable widgets and live views
- Automated alerts and notifications
- Policy-based decision tools
- Predictive insights for weather hazards

Everything is designed to help you quickly answer:\
**"Are we safe right now, and what should we do next?"**

## Core Features

### Dashboards & Views

Create and manage weather views for specific locations.

- Monitor lightning, radar, and active weather hazards
- View real-time conditions and trends
- Customize views for your organization’s needs

### Shareable Widgets

ArcWeather allows you to create **live, shareable widgets and views** that can be distributed to:

- Staff and teams
- Event coordinators
- Stakeholders and leadership
- The public (if desired)

These views update in real time and require no technical setup for end users.

### Alerts & Notifications

Stay informed with **automated alerts** based on your defined conditions.

Notifications can be delivered via:

- Email
- SMS / text message
- Push notifications

Alerts are designed to be timely, clear, and actionable.

### Policy-Based Alerts

Define your own weather safety rules and thresholds.

Examples include:

- Lightning within a defined radius
- Severe weather warnings
- Wind, heat, or other environmental thresholds

ArcWeather continuously monitors conditions and triggers alerts when your policies are met.

### Predictive Insights

ArcWeather includes **predictive and forward-looking insights** to help you prepare, not just react.

- Short-term hazard prediction
- Trend-based risk awareness
- Early indicators of changing conditions

These tools help you make proactive decisions with greater confidence.

## Who is ArcWeather For?

ArcWeather is designed for a wide range of users, including:

- Schools and universities
- Sports teams and event organizers
- Churches and community organizations
- Operations and safety teams
- Weather-aware businesses and developers

Whether you need simple visibility or advanced automation, ArcWeather scales to your needs.

## Getting Started

To begin using ArcWeather:

1. Create an account at:\
   [https://app.arcweather.cloud](https://app.arcweather.cloud)
2. Set up your first location or view
3. Configure alerts and notification preferences
4. Share your dashboards or widgets with your team

You can be up and running in just a few minutes.

## Using the API

If you are a developer and need direct access to raw weather data, ArcWeather also provides a dedicated API.

The API is separate from the platform and is intended for:

- Custom applications
- Backend integrations
- Advanced data workflows

Refer to the [**ArcWeather API Documentation**](/api-getting-started) for more information.
